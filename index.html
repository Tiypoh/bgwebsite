About

Bella's serves amazing gelato, house made waffle cones, affogatos,
sorbettinos, espresso, organic cold brew iced coffee, smoothies,
root beer floats, lemonade and fresh fudge made in house!

If you are looking for the authentic taste of gelato
and a healthier treat stop by and compare!
